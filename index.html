About Our Startup

Seiva Robotic & Technologies is an emerging technology startup focused on Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Automation, and Research & Development. We are committed to bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industrial applications through practical and innovation-driven learning.

Our startup is built with a vision to empower students, institutions, and industries by providing hands-on technology training and smart automation solutions.
